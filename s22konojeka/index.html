😼 Website Info 😼

This website will act as a showcase of the cuteness of my cat named "Cukuriņš". Here you will be able to view my favorite pictures that I have taken of my cat in the last couple of months! When I was trying to figure out what I should make my website about, I realized that I have the perfect specimen to showcase to the whole world! These pictures have all been taken by me with my Nothing Phone 1! Most of these pictures were accidental, although I did try to get Cukuriņš to pose for me a couple of times. These are all of my favorite ones as of now but I will definitely add more photos later on. As well ass more photos I am thinking about how to add all of the cute cat videos I have saved up.
